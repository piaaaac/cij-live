Title: About CIJ Stream

----

Text:

The CIJ Stream is a digital broadcast platform created by The Centre for Investigative Journalism for the 4th edition of the (link: https://tcij.org/logan text: CIJ Logan Symposium), featuring the following channels where you can watch:

<p class="d-flex">
<span class="scn mr-3 mt-1">1</span>
<span>Live stream of the main stage sessions on November 16-19, 2020</span>
</p>
<p class="d-flex">
<span class="scn mr-3 mt-1">2</span>
<span>Sessions: Recordings of the main stage sessions available on demand</span>
</p>
<p class="d-flex">
<span class="scn mr-3 mt-1">3</span>
<span>Open Lab: Commissioned and curated videos from the (link: https://openlab.tcij.org/ text: CIJ Open Lab) database</span>
</p>
<p class="d-flex">
<span class="scn mr-3 mt-1">4</span>
<span>Archive: Staff picks from previous editions of the CIJ Logan Symposium</span>
</p>

Follow the live coverage on Twitter: (link: https://twitter.com/cijlogan text: @cijlogan) (link: https://twitter.com/hashtag/CIJLogan2020 text: #CIJLogan2020)
Kindly supported by (link: https://www.loganfdn.org/ text: The Reva & David Logan Foundation).
